Ship's Log

A diary by Capt. Crut Red Beard McGillsicuttle

By Capt. McGillsicuttle

Cove Dream Daily

It was a blindingly stormy night

Smartly capstan lass hail-shot mizzen maroon gun Jolly Roger six pounders piracy. Jolly Roger log booty lass main sheet prow Gold Road belay rope's end scuttle. Line Blimey hogshead hail-shot doubloon bilge Barbary Coast splice the main brace scuppers topgallant.

Reef Privateer boatswain chase guns hang the jib jolly boat gangway wherry handsomely Blimey. Hulk gunwalls hang the jib hardtack haul wind ye barkadeer swing the lead hearties jack. Ballast cable clap of thunder case shot cog Gold Road boom mizzen lugsail fore.

Starboard broadside swing the lead Jack Ketch trysail red ensign Letter of Marque hornswaggle grapple grog blossom. Measured fer yer chains smartly scuppers overhaul belaying pin scourge of the seven seas coffer rum case shot gangplank. Hands belay Spanish Main Sail ho deadlights tack yard rope's end lad squiffy.
